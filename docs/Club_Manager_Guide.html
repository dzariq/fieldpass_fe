FieldPass — Club Manager Guide

For Club Managers: login, update club branding, and submit lineup before match day.

Important rule (Lineup deadline):

You should submit your player lineup at least 24 hours before the real match kickoff time. If you wait too late, the system/admin may lock the lineup and you may not be able to submit.

1) Login (Club Manager)

Club managers use the admin portal with Malaysia (+60) mobile OTP — the same flow as association admins (not username/password).

  1. Open the admin login page in your browser (example: http://localhost:8000/admin/login).
  2. Enter your mobile number (national digits only, after +60 — no spaces).
  3. Click Send OTP. You will receive a one-time code (SMS/WhatsApp, depending on integration).
  4. Enter the OTP code, optionally tick Remember me, then click Verify & Sign In.

The phone number must match the one stored on your admin account. If login fails, ask your association admin to confirm your number under Admins → Edit.

2) Update Club Logo

  1. From the left sidebar, go to Clubs.
  2. Find your club and click Edit.
  3. In the Avatar / Logo section:
    • Click Choose File and select a PNG/JPG image.
    • Keep it square if possible (recommended for clean display in tables and headers).
  4. Click Save / Update.

3) Update Club Jersey (Home/Away)

If your system shows jersey uploads (or “kit” images), update them the same way as the logo.

  1. Go to Clubs → Edit your club.
  2. Locate the Jersey / Kit fields (Home / Away).
  3. Upload images (PNG/JPG) and click Save / Update.

4) Submit Player Lineup (24 hours before match)

  1. From the sidebar, go to Matches (or Match).
  2. Find the upcoming match for your club.
  3. Open the match and look for Lineup / Player Lineup / Submit Lineup.
  4. Select players for:
    • Starting XI
    • Substitutes (if the competition requires it)
  5. Double-check:
    • Positions look correct (Goalkeeper / Defender / Midfielder / Forward).
    • No missing required slots.
  6. Click Save / Submit Lineup.

5) Common issues & fixes

A) I can’t see my club in the Clubs list

  • Your admin account may not be assigned to the club yet. Ask the association admin/superadmin to assign your club under Edit Admin → Assign Clubs.

B) I can’t submit lineup / lineup is empty

  • Check the competition has active clubs joined.
  • Confirm your club has players assigned (Players → filter by your club).
  • If it’s within 24 hours of kickoff, the match may be locked.

C) Logo doesn’t show after upload

  • Refresh the page (Ctrl+F5).
  • Make sure the file is JPG/PNG and under the size limit.

D) Players asking how to log in

  • Players use the player portal (example: /player/login), not the admin URL.
  • They sign in with the same +60 mobile OTP flow; their phone must match their player profile and status must be ACTIVE.
